Hello[he-loh, huh-, hel-oh]


1 (used to express a greeting, answer a telephone, or attract attention.)

2 (an exclamation of surprise, wonder, elation, etc.)

3 (used derisively to question the comprehension, intelligence, or common sense of the person being addressed): You're gonna go out with him ? Hello! noun, plural hel·los.

4 the call “hello” (used as an expression of greeting): She gave me a warm hello.

5 verb (used without object), hel·loed, hel·lo·ing. to say “hello”; to cry or shout.
